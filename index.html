Welcome to Your Anointed Journey

A guided path for new believers in Jesus Christ — Connect, Cultivate, Contribute, and Commission.

4 Stages of Your Journey

Connect

Start your relationship with Jesus Christ. Discover God's love and grace through fellowship and teaching.

Cultivate

Grow in your faith through prayer, Bible study, and mentorship. Let your roots grow deep in God’s Word.

Contribute

Serve with joy. Find your purpose in the body of Christ and use your gifts to build up others.

Commission

Go and share. Live out your calling to make disciples and bring the light of Christ to the world.
